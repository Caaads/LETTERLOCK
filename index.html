How to Play LetterLock

Objective

Choose a letter and come up with valid words starting with that letter. Have more lives than your opponent when one of you runs out of words.

Game Flow

  1. Pick Phase: Choose a letter from A-Z within the time limit
  2. Reveal Phase: Your letter and your opponent's letter are revealed
  3. Challenge Phase: Type a valid word starting with your assigned letter before time runs out
  4. Result Phase: Compare words and see who loses a life

Rules

  • Words must be at least 4 letters long
  • Words must be valid English words
  • Words cannot start with vowels (A, E, I, O, U) in harder modes
  • You lose a life if you can't submit a valid word or if your opponent's word is longer
  • Game ends when a player runs out of lives

Tips

  • Plan common words for each letter beforehand
  • Type quickly to submit before the timer ends
  • Watch out for letter repetition based on cooldown settings
